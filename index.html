Emre Kurtoğlu

About Me

ML researcher with a strong background focused on signal processing, computer vision, and fundamental ML/DL research. I bring over 6 years of experience in end-to-end ML/DL workflows, encompassing experiment design, real-world dataset curation, preprocessing, deep learning model design and training, evaluation, and deployment.

My work extends to ML/DL applications across various sensors, including FMCW radars, RGB-D/IR cameras, ECG, and iEEG. I am deeply passionate about computer vision, health sensing, and fundamental machine learning research.
